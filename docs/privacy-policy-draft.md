# nestboard — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 27 June 2026

nestboard ("the Service") is a family chore, routine, and household dashboard
operated by TABVenturing, LLC ("we", "us"). This policy explains what we collect,
why, and your choices. nestboard is currently in **beta**.

## Who uses nestboard

nestboard is set up and managed by a **parent or guardian** ("the account
holder"). The account holder creates a family and enters information about their
household, including their children. Children do not create accounts and we do
not knowingly collect information directly from children.

## What we collect

- **Account information** — your email address and authentication details, handled by our auth provider (Clerk).
- **Family information** — family name, a family URL identifier (slug), and a parent PIN you set.
- **Children's information you enter** — each child's first name (or nickname), chosen icon/color, and their chores, routines, zones, rewards, token balances, and screen-time activity.
- **Household content** — grocery lists, notes, meal plans, and announcements you add.
- **Calendar data** — if you subscribe to a calendar via an iCal URL, we store that URL and periodically fetch event details (read-only) to display them. We never write to your calendar.
- **Approximate location** — a city/location you choose, used only to show local weather.
- **Notifications** — if you enable push notifications, the device push subscription needed to deliver them.
- **Technical data** — basic logs needed to operate and secure the Service.

We do **not** sell your data, show ads, or use it for advertising.

## How we use it

To provide the dashboard and its features (chores, approvals, screen time,
calendar, weather, notifications), to secure and maintain the Service, and to
respond to your support and feedback requests.

## Who we share it with (service providers)

We use a small number of processors solely to run the Service:

- **Clerk** — authentication.
- **Railway** — application hosting and database.
- **Browser push services** (Apple, Google, Mozilla) — to deliver notifications you enable.
- **Open-Meteo** — to fetch the forecast for your chosen location (your browser requests it directly using the coordinates you set; no API key, no account).
- **Your calendar provider** — we fetch the iCal feed you subscribe to (read-only).

We do not otherwise share personal information except where required by law.

## Children's privacy

nestboard stores information **about** children, entered by the account holder
who is their parent or guardian. We do not let children create accounts and do
not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. A child's
personal page is reachable only via a private link controlled by the account
holder. If you believe a child's information has been collected improperly,
contact us at tabventuring@gmail.com and we will delete it.

## Data retention and deletion

We keep your data while your family is active. You can request deletion of your
family and all associated data at any time by contacting tabventuring@gmail.com or
using the in-app deletion request; we will remove it promptly.

## Security

We use authentication, per-family data isolation, and rate limiting. No system
is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your data and limit access to it.

## Your choices

You can edit or remove children, chores, calendars, and other content in the
app, disable notifications per device, and request full deletion.

## Changes

We may update this policy as the Service evolves. Material changes will be
surfaced in the app.

## Contact

Questions or requests: **tabventuring@gmail.com**.
